[align=center][b][p]Regional Communications Act[/p][/b][/align]


[align=center][i][p]An act clarifying proper communication by government officials[/p][/i][/align]


[b][anchor=a1][/anchor][p]1. Scope[/p][/b]


[anchor=a1_s1][/anchor][p](1) Modes of mass communication covered include[/p]
[anchor=a1_s1_a][/anchor][p]a. Mass Telegrams to the region using Regional Officer powers[/p]
[anchor=a1_s1_b][/anchor][p]b. Dispatches pinned to the regional World Factbook Entry[/p]
[anchor=a1_s1_c][/anchor][p]c. Changes to the regional World Factbook Entry[/p]
[anchor=a1_s1_d][/anchor][p]d. Any other form of communication, apart from the Regional Message Board, that is directly visible on the NationStates website by the in-game region at large.Â [/p]


[anchor=a1_s2][/anchor][p](2) Any individual capable of engaging in mass communication with the region as described above shall be subject to the regulations contained herein.[/p]



[b][anchor=a2][/anchor][p]2. Mass Communication[/p][/b]


[anchor=a2_s1][/anchor][p](1) Communications to the region shall be[/p]
[anchor=a2_s1_a][/anchor][p]a. respectful[/p]
[anchor=a2_s1_b][/anchor][p]b. honest[/p]
[anchor=a2_s1_c][/anchor][p]c. restrained to a reasonable frequency of notifications[/p]
[anchor=a2_s1_d][/anchor][p]d. neutral with respect to political ideology or debate[/p]


[anchor=a2_s2][/anchor][p](2) Communications to the region shall not[/p]
[anchor=a2_s2_a][/anchor][p]a. mislead the public[/p]
[anchor=a2_s2_b][/anchor][p]b. attack or otherwise diminish other officials[/p]
[anchor=a2_s2_c][/anchor][p]c. attack or otherwise diminish The South Pacific or her allies[/p]
[anchor=a2_s2_d][/anchor][p]d. directly or indirectly threaten the security apparatus of the South Pacific or her allies[/p]
[anchor=a2_s2_e][/anchor][p]e. deliberately influence a regional election in favour of or against specific candidates[/p]


[anchor=a2_s3][/anchor][p](3) The Delegate may, at their discretion, enact further restrictions, guidelines, or approval processes on the use of mass communications.[/p]


[anchor=a2_s4][/anchor][p](4) The Council on Regional Security may temporarily suspend the provisions contained herein for themselves or any duly chosen surrogates for purposes of regional security.[/p]



[b][anchor=a3][/anchor][p]3. Infractions[/p][/b]


[anchor=a3_s1][/anchor][p](1) Infractions may be brought before the High Court in a court case. For the duration of the court proceedings, the Delegate may suspend the mass communication capabilities of the accused.
	[/p]
